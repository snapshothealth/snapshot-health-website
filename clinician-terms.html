Scope of Services

SnapShot Health offers Customer one of two service programs, as selected on the Customer's Service Order Form:

  • Remote Concierge Care — A fully turnkey program in which SnapShot Health's Care Concierge team performs patient enrollment, device fulfillment, daily clinical monitoring, interactive patient communications, CMS-compliant time tracking, clinical documentation, and delivery of billing-ready reports to Customer. Fees are charged per CPT/HCPCS code rendered, subject to the Performance Guarantee in Section 5.
  • Software Platform — A self-managed program in which SnapShot Health provides Customer access to the SnapShot Health platform and, at Customer's election, connected medical devices. Customer's staff performs enrollment, monitoring, patient communications, time tracking, and documentation using the platform. Fees are charged on a flat per-billable-patient-per-month basis as set forth in the Service Order Form. The Software Platform program is offered in two tiers: (i) Software Platform — No Device, at a flat rate per billable patient per month, with Customer supplying its own FDA-cleared devices; and (ii) Software Platform — With Device, at a higher flat rate per billable patient per month, which includes one SnapShot Health-supplied connected device per enrolled patient.

For purposes of Software Platform pricing, “billable patient” means any patient enrolled on the SnapShot Health platform who transmitted at least one (1) physiologic reading during the applicable calendar month. The Software Platform flat fee applies regardless of whether the patient met CMS billing thresholds, and the Performance Guarantee in Section 5 does not apply to the Software Platform program.

Both programs cover the following service categories, as ordered by Customer:

  • Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) — CPT 99453, 99454, 99445, 99457, 99458, 99470
  • Chronic Care Management (CCM) — CPT 99490, 99439
  • Complex CCM — CPT 99487, 99489
  • Behavioral Health Integration (BHI) — CPT 99484
  • Advanced Primary Care Management (APCM) — HCPCS G0556, G0557, G0558, G0568, G0569, G0570

Remote Concierge Care services include: Texas Medicaid eligibility verification, patient enrollment and consent workflow support, device fulfillment, daily clinical monitoring by our Care Concierge team, CMS-compliant time tracking and clinical notes, clinical documentation delivered into Customer's EHR, monthly billing-ready reports with supporting audit records, patient education, onboarding, and remote staff training for Customer's team.

Software Platform services include: Access to the SnapShot Health platform for the Customer's designated users; a practice portal for patient management; data transmission and HIPAA-compliant infrastructure; device shipping and replacement (for the With Device tier only); and standard onboarding and platform training for Customer's team. Software Platform does not include Care Concierge clinical labor, patient engagement calls, clinical monitoring, documentation drafting, Texas Medicaid eligibility verification, EHR integration services, or billing-ready reports prepared by SnapShot Health.

Customer is responsible for claim submission and reimbursement. SnapShot Health does not submit claims, bill payers, or receive reimbursement on Customer's behalf under either program.

Performance Guarantee

★ You Only Pay When Patients Are Billable.

Scope. This Performance Guarantee applies exclusively to Customers enrolled in SnapShot Health's Remote Concierge Care program, under which SnapShot Health's Care Concierge team performs patient engagement, monitoring, clinical time tracking, and documentation activities. Customers enrolled in the Software Platform program (either No Device or With Device tier) are charged a flat per-billable-patient-per-month fee as described in Section 1 and the Service Order Form, and this Performance Guarantee does not apply to Software Platform fees.

For Remote Concierge Care Customers, Customer is not invoiced for any enrolled patient who does not meet the applicable CMS billing threshold for a given code. Specifically:

Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM):

  • CPT 99453 (RPM Setup): Not billed if the patient does not complete device setup and at least 2 days of data transmission.
  • CPT 99454 (Device Supply, 16+ days): Not billed if the patient does not transmit data on at least 16 days in a 30-day period.
  • CPT 99445 (Device Supply, 2–15 days — NEW 2026): Not billed if the patient does not transmit data on at least 2 days in a 30-day period. Mutually exclusive with 99454 in the same period.
  • CPT 99457 (Treatment Management, 20+ min): Not billed if the required 20 minutes of clinical time and at least one interactive patient communication in the calendar month are not met.
  • CPT 99458 (Treatment Management, each additional 20 min): Not billed if the additional 20-minute threshold beyond 99457 is not met.
  • CPT 99470 (Treatment Management, 10–19 min — NEW 2026): Not billed if the required 10 minutes of clinical time and at least one interactive patient communication in the calendar month are not met. Mutually exclusive with 99457 in the same calendar month.

Chronic Care Management (CCM) & Complex CCM:

  • CPT 99490 (CCM Initial, 20 min): Not billed if the required 20 minutes of non-face-to-face care coordination by clinical staff in the calendar month are not met.
  • CPT 99439 (CCM, each additional 20 min): Not billed if the additional 20-minute threshold beyond 99490 is not met.
  • CPT 99487 (Complex CCM Initial, 60 min): Not billed if the required 60 minutes of clinical staff time with moderate- or high-complexity medical decision-making are not met.
  • CPT 99489 (Complex CCM, each additional 30 min): Not billed if the additional 30-minute threshold beyond 99487 is not met.

Behavioral Health Integration (BHI) & Advanced Primary Care Management (APCM):

  • CPT 99484 (BHI): Not billed if the required 20 minutes of behavioral health care management in the calendar month are not met.
  • HCPCS G0556, G0557, G0558 (APCM Levels 1–3): Not billed if required visit, care plan, or documentation standards are not met.
  • HCPCS G0568, G0569, G0570 (APCM + CoCM / BHI combinations — NEW 2026): Not billed if required time, documentation, or interactive communication standards for the applicable combined service are not met.

Eligibility & payer exclusions:

  • Texas Medicaid patients: Not billed for any patient SnapShot Health's eligibility verification determines qualifies for the Texas Medicaid program.

Denial refunds. If Customer's biller receives a denial of reimbursement for any patient where SnapShot Health was paid, and Customer provides a copy of the Explanation of Benefits (EOB), SnapShot Health will credit the corresponding fee on the next invoice.

Term & Termination

5.1 Initial Term

Customer elects one of the following terms on the Service Order Form:

  • Month-to-month: Services continue on a rolling monthly basis until terminated. Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice.
  • 12-Month Commitment: Initial term of 12 months with a 5% discount on all CPT rates. Automatically renews for successive 12-month terms unless either party provides 60 days' written notice prior to renewal. May be terminated for material breach per Section 6.2.

5.2 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach if the breach remains uncured after:

  • Monetary breach: 10 calendar days following written notice.
  • Non-monetary breach: 30 calendar days following written notice.

5.3 Effects of Termination

  • Customer shall pay all fees owed for services rendered through the termination date.
  • Customer shall cease use of the SnapShot Health platform and return any leased devices within 30 days.
  • SnapShot Health will cooperate with orderly transition and provide patient data export upon request and in accordance with HIPAA.
  • Sections relating to confidentiality, indemnification, limitation of liability, data, payment obligations, and governing law survive termination.
